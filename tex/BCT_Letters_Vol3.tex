\begin{abstract}
This volume collects thirty-nine BCT Letters spanning cosmology (black hole coupling, neutron stars, inflation, Hubble tension, dark matter, dark energy, arrow of time), astrophysics (FRBs as Hopf defects, dark PeVatrons, gravitational wave signatures), quantum foundations (measurement problem, information paradox, black hole entropy), exotic phenomena (Ferrocell topology, sonoluminescence, cosmic exhale), and the biology/persistence series (protein folding, viruses as Hopf defects, the Persistence Theorem, the Cosmic Persistence Theorem extending to consciousness). Letter~70 (the Cosmic Persistence Theorem) is new, written 20~March~2026, extending the Persistence Theorem of Letter~69 to galactic, cosmic, and cognitive scales. All results use zero free parameters. Companion appendices are in Zenodo Records~3 and~4.
\end{abstract}
