\begin{abstract}
This volume collects sixteen BCT Letters that were not included in the original Day~1 and Day~2 Zenodo uploads. They cover the Standard Model extension programme (Letters~13--17: dark matter, neutrinos, baryogenesis, CMB, muon~$g$-2) and the precision derivation programme (Letters~23--35: the BCT scale triangle, deconfinement temperature, Weinberg angle, full GR, GP axiom, proton mass, NLO corrections, neutron--proton mass difference, electron mass, QCD scale, and Newton's constant). All results use zero free parameters. Companion appendices are in Zenodo Records~3 and~4.
\end{abstract}
