## Access Tiers

### Free Humanitarian Access
Any organisation working on clean water, food security, energy access, environmental protection, or public health may use BCT-PEACE-SUITE technologies at no cost.

### The Birdseed Clause
Any individual or community that feeds wildlife qualifies for free access. This is not a joke — it is a founding ethical principle of the BCT programme.

### Commercial Licensing
Commercial use requires a licence. All commercial licence fees fund the BCT Foundation, a not-for-profit entity dedicated to distributing these technologies where they are needed most.
